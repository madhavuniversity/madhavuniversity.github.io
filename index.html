About Our Department

Pioneering agricultural education since 1985

Welcome to the Department of Agriculture

The Department of Agriculture at Madhav University is committed to excellence in agricultural education, research, and extension. For over three decades, we have been preparing students to become leaders in the agricultural industry, research institutions, and policy-making bodies.

Our faculty members are internationally recognized experts in their fields, conducting cutting-edge research that addresses critical challenges in food security, sustainable agriculture, and environmental conservation.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a hands-on learning approach, we provide students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving field of agriculture.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-art infrastructure for learning and research

World-Class Learning Environment

The Department of Agriculture boasts exceptional facilities that provide students with hands-on experience and support cutting-edge research:

  • Modern laboratories equipped with advanced analytical instruments
  • 200-acre experimental farm with diverse cropping systems
  • Greenhouses and polyhouses for controlled environment studies
  • Plant tissue culture and biotechnology laboratory
  • Meteorological observatory for weather and climate studies
  • Soil and water testing laboratory
  • Digital classroom and computer lab with agricultural software
  • Agricultural machinery and equipment workshop
